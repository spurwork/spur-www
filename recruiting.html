Questions?

Frequently asked

How is this different from a recruiting platform?

Platforms give you tools to do recruiting yourself. We actually do the recruiting for you. No software to learn, no dashboards to navigate, no data entry. We find candidates, screen them, and deliver them to your inbox.

What's the pricing model?

$2,000/month for unlimited recruiting support. No per-hire fees, no seat licenses. Pay annually for $20,000 ($4,000 savings). We work hand-in-hand with your HR team to fill positions. Cancel monthly anytime.

How do I request a new position?

Text us, email us, or call us. Seriously, that's it. Say "I need a math teacher" and we start working. No forms, no portals, no tickets to submit.

How do you vet candidates?

Every candidate goes through credential verification, reference checks, and an initial screening interview. We only send you candidates who meet your requirements and are genuinely interested in your district.

What about hard-to-fill positions?

That's where we really shine. SpEd, bilingual, STEM: these are the positions where passive job postings fail. We actively hunt for these candidates using targeted outreach and referral networks.

How do I get reports?

Just ask. Text "pipeline update" for an instant summary. Email us to set up automated weekly reports. Call if you need something custom. We deliver data how you want it, when you want it.
