Why This Scholarship

Great teachers are at the center of great schools

At Spur, we work alongside schools every day and see firsthand the impact that passionate, dedicated teachers have on students and communities. We also see the growing teacher shortage across the country.

The Spur Teacher Scholarship was created to support high school students who are ready to answer the call to teach. Whether you want to lead a kindergarten classroom, coach a high school team, or inspire students in STEM — we want to help you get there.

This $5,000 scholarship will be awarded to one outstanding student to put toward their college education in a teaching or education-related field.

Overview

The Spur Teacher Scholarship awards $5,000 to a high school junior or senior who plans to pursue a career in teaching. The goal is simple: make the path to becoming a teacher a little easier.

Eligibility

Applicants must:

  • Be a current high school junior or senior (Class of 2026 or 2027) and a legal resident of the United States
  • Have a cumulative unweighted GPA of 2.5 or higher on a 4.0 scale
  • Plan to enroll in an accredited college or university for the upcoming academic year
  • Intend to pursue a degree or pathway related to teaching or education

Application Requirements

Applicants must submit:

  • Basic personal and school information
  • Short written responses to the application questions

All submissions must be original work.

Selection Criteria

Applications will be reviewed based on:

  • Demonstrated commitment to becoming a teacher
  • Thoughtfulness and clarity in responses
  • Evidence of helping others learn or grow
  • Overall strength of the application

Finalists may be contacted for additional information.

Timeline

  • Application deadline: May 1, 2026
  • Winner announced: June 1, 2026

Late or incomplete applications will not be considered.

Award Distribution

The $5,000 scholarship will be paid directly to the recipient's accredited college or university and applied toward tuition and required fees for the fall semester following the award.

If funds remain after tuition and required fees are covered, the remaining balance may be provided to the recipient for education-related expenses, such as books, supplies, or housing.

Use of Funds

Funds are intended for education-related expenses. Spur is not responsible for how funds are ultimately used once disbursed in accordance with these terms.

Verification

The selected recipient must provide proof of enrollment before funds are distributed. If the recipient does not enroll, Spur reserves the right to select an alternate recipient.

Publicity

By applying, applicants agree that Spur may use their name, likeness, and application materials (in whole or in part) for promotional purposes, including announcing the winner.

General

  • Spur reserves the right to modify or cancel the scholarship at any time
  • Decisions are final and not subject to appeal
  • No purchase or payment is required to apply
