Questions?

Frequently asked

How is this different from other after-school providers?

We lead with academic outcomes, not just enrichment. Our programs are validated by third-party research showing 10.6% higher academic growth. Most providers focus on supervision and activities. We focus on measurable results.

What does "turn-key" actually mean?

It means minimal lift for your team. We handle all staffing (hiring, training, management), provide the curriculum, manage enrollment and communication, and track outcomes. Your team approves the plan and sees the results. We do everything else.

How do you measure academic impact?

We use i-Ready comparisons and third-party validated assessments. You'll see real data on student growth, not just participation numbers. Our impact reports show exactly how students are progressing.

Can you align with our existing curriculum?

Yes. Our programs are designed to reinforce your school-day instruction. We work with your team to understand priorities and align our activities to support what's happening in the classroom.

What's the implementation timeline?

Most districts are up and running within 4-6 weeks. We move fast because we know students can't wait. Summer programs typically need 8-12 weeks lead time for staffing and planning.

How does funding work?

We work with a variety of funding sources including ESSER, 21st CCLC, Title I, state grants, and district budgets. Our team can help you identify eligible funding and build a sustainable model.
