What we do

A recruiting pipeline that runs without you.

We handle the recruiting and interviewing. You make the final call. Here's how it breaks down:

✓ We build and run targeted recruiting campaigns in your area to attract CDL holders and trainee candidates.
✓ We screen every applicant so you only see people who are actually qualified and interested.
✓ We interview every candidate so you only meet people who are qualified, interested, and ready to start.
✓ You make the final hiring decision. They're your employees, on your payroll, wearing your badge.
No software to learn. No system to set up. No extra work on your plate. We plug in and start delivering candidates.
How it works

Three steps. That's it.

1

We launch your pipeline

We learn your needs, your area, and your requirements. Then we build and launch recruiting campaigns targeting bus drivers near your district.

2

We screen and interview

Applicants come in. We screen for CDL status, availability, and background, then interview the top candidates. You only hear about the ones worth your time.

3

You make the call

We interview candidates and present the best fits. You make the final hiring decision and bring them onto your team.

Common questions

Do you employ the drivers?

No. You hire every driver directly onto your team. They're your employees, on your payroll. We just find them for you.

How quickly can we start?

Most districts are up and running within a week. We'll do a quick intake call to learn your requirements, then launch recruiting campaigns right away.

How does the partnership work?

We work on an annual partnership basis. This gives us the runway to build a sustained recruiting pipeline for your district — not just a one-time push.

What if we don't hire anyone?

You only pay the per-hire fee when you actually hire someone. The monthly fee covers the recruiting campaigns and candidate pipeline work regardless of hires.

How is this different from a staffing company?

A staffing company sends you their employees on a temporary basis and charges a markup on every hour. We find and interview candidates who become your permanent employees. No markup on wages, no middleman once they're hired.
